Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?

TrainerHub uses passwordless email sign-in. Enter your email on the Sign In screen, tap Send Code, then enter the 6-digit code we email you. There is no password to remember.

I didn't receive my login code

  • Check your spam or junk folder.
  • Make sure you entered the same email you originally signed up with.
  • Codes expire after 10 minutes — request a new one if needed.
  • Still stuck? Email support@trainerhub.app and we'll help.

How do I book a session with my trainer?

Open your trainer's profile, tap Book a session, pick an available time, and confirm. You'll get a notification when your trainer accepts the request.

How do trainers get paid?

Trainers connect a Stripe account in the app. Payouts are processed automatically after each completed session, on Stripe's standard schedule.

How do I cancel or reschedule a session?

Open the session from your Schedule and tap Cancel or Reschedule. Please give your trainer as much notice as possible — individual trainers may have their own cancellation policy.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or email support@trainerhub.app from your account email and we will permanently delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

Privacy & data

We use Supabase and Stripe — both industry-standard providers — to keep your data and payments secure. See our Privacy Policy for details on what we collect and how it's used.
