Who We Are

More than a church.
A community.

"The Remedy Church exists to teach Christ boldly, love our city deeply, and build lasting and strategic partnerships that transform lives."

Rooted in downtown Akron, Ohio, we are a community built on three pillars: Love Christ. Love People. Build Community. You don't have to have it all together to show up — that's the whole point.
