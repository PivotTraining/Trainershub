Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in? TrainerHub supports two sign-in methods: a password, or a one-time code sent to your email. On the sign-in screen, tap "Get a one-time code instead" to switch methods.
I didn't receive my one-time code. Check your spam folder. Codes arrive within a minute. If repeated requests fail, wait 60 minutes (a rate limit applies) or email us.
How do I switch between trainer and client mode? On the sign-in screen, use the "I'm a client / I'm a trainer" toggle before signing in. Your role is set when your account is created.
How do trainers get paid? Trainers connect a Stripe account from Profile → Payouts. Stripe handles all payments — TrainerHub never touches your bank details.
How do I delete my account? Open the app, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, sessions, and any associated data. You can also email support@trainerhub.app to request deletion.
How do I cancel or reschedule a session? Open the Bookings tab, tap the session, and choose Cancel or Reschedule. Trainers and clients both receive a notification.

Privacy & data

Read our Privacy Policy for details on how account data is stored and used. We use Supabase (data) and Stripe (payments); no data is sold to third parties.

App Review note

If you are an Apple App Review reviewer and cannot access trainer functionality, the demo credentials in the App Review Information section of App Store Connect log you into a fully-populated trainer account. You may also tap "Continue as Demo Trainer" on the sign-in screen.
